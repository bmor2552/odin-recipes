Salad

Time Strawberry Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, cut or torn into bite-size pieces
  • 1 (10 ounce) package shredded cabbage
  • 1 ½ cups golden raisins
  • 1 cup red grapes, halved
  • 1 pint strawberries, sliced
  • ½ red onion, sliced

Steps

  1. Soak all of your ingredients in a big bowl filled with water vinegar and baking soda for 15 minutes to clean your produce
  2. Rinse your ingredients thoroughly under cold water to remove any residue or critters
  3. Pat your produce dry with a clean kitchen towel and place them in a bowl
  4. Rough chop each ingredient and place them in a large mixing bowl
  5. Grab a pair of tongs and mix the salad well
  6. Enjoy!!!
