PB & J

The Best PB & J In The World

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of wonder bread
  • jiff's chunky peanut butter
  • grape jelly

Steps

  1. Toast you 2 slices of bread until golden
  2. Gently spread the chunky peanut butter on one slice
  3. Gently spread the grape jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Carefully place the chucky peanut butter slice on top of the grape jelly slice

And there you have it PB & J!!
